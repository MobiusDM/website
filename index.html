About Möbius

We understand the frustration of managing multiple tools for different systems. Why should you need separate solutions when they serves the same purpose?

Our solution brings true unification. Declarative configuration that works everywhere, API-first architecture for seamless integration, and the flexibility to manage your entire environment from one place.

Platform Agnostic

No more choosing between systems. Our solution works everywhere, giving every platform the attention it deserves.

Declarative Control

Define your desired state once, apply it everywhere. Same configuration language across all environments.

API-First Design

Built for integration. Native API, CLI tools, and PowerShell modules. Automate everything, integrate with anything.

Air-Gap Compatible

Deploy on-premises or in air-gapped environments. No cloud dependencies, no forced internet connectivity—your infrastructure, your rules.

Data Sovereignty

Run on-premises or in any cloud, in any country. Your data stays where you want it, complying with local regulations and sovereignty requirements.
